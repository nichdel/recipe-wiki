* 1/2 cup brown sugar

* 1/4 cup flour

* 1/4 cup butter

===Directions===

# Mix all together to form a crumble

==Batter==

===Ingredients===

* 1 1/2 cup flour

* 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

* 1/2 teaspoon salt

* 1 egg

* 3/4 cup sugar

* 1/3 cup butter

* 1/2 cup milk 

* 1 teaspoon vanilla

* 9 teaspoon dark cocoa powder (optional) 

===Directions===

# Mix all ingredients
# Pour batter into a greased pan and top with crumble topping
# Bake 375 degrees for 25-30 minutes
